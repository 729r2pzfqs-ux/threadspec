How M42×2.0 Dimensions Are Calculated

All dimensions on this page are calculated from ISO 724 formulas using the nominal diameter (42 mm) and pitch (2.0 mm):

  • Fundamental triangle height: H = (√3/2) × P = 1.7321 mm
  • Pitch diameter: d₂ = d − 0.6495 × P = 40.701 mm
  • Minor diameter (external): d₁ = d − 1.2269 × P = 39.5462 mm
  • Minor diameter (internal): D₁ = d − 1.0825 × P = 39.835 mm
  • Thread depth: 5H/8 = 1.0825 mm
  • Tensile stress area: Aₛ = (π/4) × ((d₂ + d₃)/2)² = 1255.33 mm²
