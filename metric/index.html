About ISO Metric Coarse Threads

ISO metric coarse threads (ISO 261) are the default thread pitch for each nominal diameter. When a thread is specified as simply 'M10', it means M10 coarse (M10×1.5). Coarse threads are preferred for general-purpose fastening because they are more tolerant of minor damage and easier to assemble.

All dimensions are calculated using ISO 724 formulas. Pitch diameter: d₂ = d − 0.6495P. Minor diameter: d₁ = d − 1.2269P. Tap drill sizes are based on the internal minor diameter (D₁).
